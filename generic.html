Some conceptual stuff

In this section I talk more about the way I represent my ideas and how I translate them into pieces.

multifunctionality

Multifunctionality can be created from 15 well thought-out cuts on a meter of fabric and without the need to sew.

traces of you

A love relationship is vibrating at the same frequency, being of the same color having different shades. These, leave us a mark that we carry impregnated to us, a trace, either in form of memories or in form of an ephemeral hug that becomes eternal...

This piece aims to communicate the impact that important people have on our lives through a hug, the marks are made with the heat of another body and the pressure generated by hugging. After a few minutes the piece returns to normal, ready for another new trail.

"If the coat fits you..."

Many times we carry coats full of feelings that overflow us, that deform us and that create voids that we then try to fill; such as sadness, insecurities or fears of all colors. These only stop weighing us until we realize that they are not our size.

I apply the deconstruction method with some ties and a coat.

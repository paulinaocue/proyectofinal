    ABOUT ME

    I’m a fashion student at CENTRO university, based in Mexico city. I consider myself an intelligent, creative, intense and very passionate person, almost everything I do is based in my emotions. I'm good at finding solutions to problems and adapting. I really enjoy my career and I can't imagine myself studying anything else

      INTERESTS

      I want to put into practice all the knowledge acquired during my career and face new challenges in order to progress both professionally and personally.

      ASPIRATIONS

      For now, my closest aspirations are to finish my career, do a master's degree in coolhunting and start doing internships and jobs that help me have more experience in the fashion world.

      VALUES

      I would like to work on honest projects that have a friendly impact on the environment, that are fair, without racism or misogyny, that have the objective of helping others or that generate creative proposals for social or cultural development.

      WHAT IS FASHION DESIGN?

      Studying fashion is like having a superpower. With it we can see through people. We can change shape, the way we think, find an identity or change it completely, return to the past or go to the future. Fashion is eternal. Always reborn. It is transformed according to the society and at the same time causes a transformation. Is a language, is history, is also economy, is one of the biggest industries in the world, so many jobs are developed in this area. For me everything is related to fashion.
